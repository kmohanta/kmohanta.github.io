I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Mathematical Analysis at Charles University, Prague. My reseach revolves around fractional Sobolev spaces and related embeddings/ functional inequalities (Hardy, Poincare, Sobolev etc). My recent interests are in Sobolev extension problems, quasiconformal composition operators and limiting embedding of fractional Sobolev spaces.
