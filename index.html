I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics and Computer Science at Indian Institute of Technology Goa, India. My reseach revolves around fractional Sobolev spaces and related embeddings/ functional inequalities (Hardy, Poincare, Sobolev etc). My recent interests are in Sobolev extension problems, quasiconformal composition operators and limiting embedding of fractional Sobolev spaces.
